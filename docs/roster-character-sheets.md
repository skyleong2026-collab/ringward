# Ringward — Roster Character Sheets (draft v0)

The legibility/attachment pass. Each unit gets a **role** (its job — the thing you
read at a glance), a **vibe** (personality so there's something to bond to), a
**one-line fiction** (who it is in the world), and a **look hook** (the silhouette
to draw against). Names are deliberately *downstream* — once the role + image land,
the name should fall out of them.

Tone: folk-mystery. Grunlings are grown earthen-machines with a glowing core for a
heart (GDD §21). The look hooks should read in silhouette first.

> Status: **DRAFT for Sky's review.** Roles are wired into the game (card +
> PreBattle tags). Vibe / fiction / look / name are proposals — nothing locked.

## Guardians — the wall
Slow, immovable, built to make the squad someone else's problem.

### vault — role: **Squad Shield**  ·  signature: Bank
- **Vibe:** the old reliable. Calm, immovable, says little, never the one who breaks.
- **Fiction:** "Stood in the same gap for seventy years. The blight went around."
- **Look:** broad and low, a hunched slab of layered stone plates; the core glows deep in a chest-cavity you can only see when it braces. A cairn that decided to stand up.
- **Name candidates:** Cairnward · Lodestone · Brimwall

### bastion — role: **Bodyguard**  ·  signature: Intercept
- **Vibe:** devoted, watchful, a little anxious — always tracking who's about to get hit.
- **Fiction:** "It does not fight for itself. It never has."
- **Look:** upright, plated like overlapping scutes, one oversized forearm it raises as a shield; core sits behind that arm, brightest when it blocks.
- **Name candidates:** Wardscale · Shieldkin · Bodyward

### bulwark — role: **Blast Damper**  ·  signature: Aegis
- **Vibe:** stoic, mossy, ancient. The one that's been rained on a thousand times and stopped caring.
- **Fiction:** "Where it stands, the air forgets how to burn."
- **Look:** rounded boulder-back furred with moss and slag-crust; a dull, banked core — light you feel more than see. Horns that look grown, not made.
- **Name candidates:** Quellhorn · Mossward · Cinderdamp

## Echos — the amplifier
Moderate everything; their power is what they do to *everyone else*.

### conduit — role: **Carry Amplifier**  ·  signature: Amplify
- **Vibe:** eager hype-creature, lives to make the star shine brighter. Loud, generous.
- **Fiction:** "Alone it's an echo. Beside a strong one, it's a second strong one."
- **Look:** lean, antenna-finned, lined with filament that lights up in a chasing pattern when an ally acts. Core strung along the spine like beads.
- **Name candidates:** Fullchorus · Resound · Loudloom

### nexus — role: **Burst Enabler**  ·  signature: Cascade
- **Vibe:** the conductor — patient, then sudden. Holds, holds, then everyone moves at once.
- **Fiction:** "It waits for the moment the whole field can move as one."
- **Look:** many-legged, web-postured, limbs that look like they connect to everything around it; core flares the instant it calls the repeat.
- **Name candidates:** Twicewing · Encore · Rebound

### link — role: **Field Medic**  ·  signature: Tether
- **Vibe:** quiet caretaker, keeps a thread on whoever's hurting. The mom of the squad.
- **Fiction:** "It feels the others fray before they do."
- **Look:** soft, thread-trailing, gossamer filaments reaching to allies; core dims slightly each time it spends itself to shield someone.
- **Name candidates:** Mendthread · Knitline · Bindwell

## Swifts — the striker
Glass. Fast, lethal, fragile — they decide who dies, not whether they're hit.

### fang — role: **Assassin**  ·  signature: Flank
- **Vibe:** cocky, impatient, allergic to the front line. Goes around, every time.
- **Fiction:** "Walls are for other people's problems."
- **Look:** low and bladed, all forward motion; a single bright core-eye, legs built for the first move of the round.
- **Name candidates:** Backbiter · Slipfang · Pastguard

### striker — role: **Shield-Breaker**  ·  signature: Sunder
- **Vibe:** patient, surgical, quiet. Finds the seam and opens it for the team.
- **Fiction:** "Found every seam in the world's armor. Then in everyone else's."
- **Look:** thin, chisel-limbed, narrow core like a bright slit; precise, economical posture — nothing wasted.
- **Name candidates:** Sunderclaw · Cracktalon · Shellsplit

### claw — role: **Closer**  ·  signature: Bloodscent
- **Vibe:** the finisher — focused, predatory, only interested in what's already falling.
- **Fiction:** "Doesn't start fights. Ends them."
- **Look:** lean hunter, low head, core that brightens as a target weakens — it literally lights up near a kill.
- **Name candidates:** Woundhound · Lastbite · Cullclaw

## Reactors — the bomb
Low HP, low starting attack; the threat is what they become if you let them cook.

### spark — role: **Reactor Battery**  ·  signature: Chain Igniter
- **Vibe:** generous spark-plug; its whole joy is lighting up its friends. The team player.
- **Fiction:** "One spark is nothing. It's never just one spark."
- **Look:** small, round, fizzing; core visibly leaks charge toward nearby Reactors like a pilot light.
- **Name candidates:** Chainspark · Kindlekin · Passflame

### flicker — role: **Sustained Burner**  ·  signature: Sputter
- **Vibe:** restless, twitchy, can't sit still — pops constantly, never the big one.
- **Fiction:** "Never the blast you fear. Always the one you can't make stop."
- **Look:** flickering, uneven glow, little vents that puff embers; core stutters bright-dim-bright.
- **Name candidates:** Stutterspark · Embertick · Popfizz

### cinder — role: **Delayed Nuke**  ·  signature: Banked Heat
- **Vibe:** ominous, patient, almost asleep — until it isn't. The one you play around.
- **Fiction:** "Patient. Patient. Then the whole room is gone."
- **Look:** heavy, banked, a deep coal-glow that brightens by degrees over the fight; long fuse-like vents. Looks dormant until the last second.
- **Name candidates:** Longfuse · Slowburn · Deepcoal
